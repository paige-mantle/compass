      I want you to help me design an onboarding automation flow for Mantle.

      My goal is to improve activation for new customers after they install my app.

      Please create a Mantle flow that:

      - targets newly installed customers
      - starts when a customer installs the app
      - sends a welcome email immediately
      - waits 2 days
      - checks whether the customer has become active / completed a key activation event
      - if they have not activated, sends a follow-up email with setup guidance
      - waits another 3 days
      - checks again whether they activated
      - if they still have not activated, creates an internal task for the team to follow up
      - if they have activated at any checkpoint, ends the flow

      Please structure the result as:

      - Flow objective
      - Recommended trigger
      - Entry conditions
      - Step-by-step flow logic
      - Branch conditions
      - Suggested email copy for each email
      - Suggested internal task title and description
      - Potential improvements or variants

      Important:

      - Keep the logic compatible with Mantle-style automations
      - Use clear, implementation-ready step names
      - Make reasonable assumptions where needed, and clearly label them
      - If "activation event" is ambiguous, suggest 3 example activation events I could use
      - Keep it practical and concise

## Overview

Use this workflow to design an onboarding automation that gets new installs to value faster — welcome, follow-up, activation check, and an internal escalation when an account stalls.

Built for app teams who want a clear post-install flow without staring at a blank automation canvas.

## Best for

- Shopify app teams
- Lifecycle marketing
- Founder-led apps
- Customer success teams
- Product-led SaaS

## Outputs

- Onboarding flow objective
- Recommended trigger + entry conditions
- Step-by-step automation logic
- Branch conditions for activation
- Welcome + follow-up email copy
- Internal task for stalled accounts
- Variants and improvement ideas
