## Overview

Use this workflow to identify review-ready merchants, define safer audience segments, and generate personalized Shopify App Store review campaigns using AI.

Built for Shopify app teams using engagement signals, lifecycle data, and customer context to improve review quality without harming the customer experience.

- Shopify app teams
- Lifecycle marketing
- Founder-led apps
- Customer success teams

- Safer exclusion logic
- Personalized review request emails
- Campaign timing recommendations
- Segmentation ideas
- Lifecycle automation ideas
