## Overview

Build a reusable operational context document your AI tools can read before drafting support replies, onboarding flows, marketing copy, or product docs.

One source of truth — about your company, product, customers, workflows, positioning, and voice — instead of rewriting the same context into every prompt.

## Best for

- Founders running AI-first ops
- Shopify app teams
- Support + lifecycle teams
- Product marketing
- AI-assisted documentation

## Outputs

- Company + product overview
- ICP and customer pain points
- Positioning and messaging guide
- Brand voice + formatting rules
- Product workflow reference
- Terminology glossary
- Reusable system-prompt foundation
