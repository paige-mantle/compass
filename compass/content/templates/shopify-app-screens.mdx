## Overview

Better Shopify App Store visuals without starting from scratch. Layout patterns, headline formulas, and the AI-assisted generation steps that win merchant comprehension.

## How it works

Drop in one of:

- App listing URL
- Landing page
- Existing screenshots
- Feature list

You get back **screen concepts**, **headline copy**, and **layout directions** — ready for a designer, a generation tool, or a product screenshot.

## What you can create

- **Bento screens** — multi-feature compositions in one frame
- **Mobile mockup screens** — phone-frame shots with overlay copy
- **Feature spotlight screens** — single-feature deep-dives
- **Workflow screens** — step-by-step "how it works" panels
- **Headline variations** — A/B-ready merchant-language headlines
